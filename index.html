Zelda: Breath of the wild

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the wild was a 2017 game released for the WiiU and Nintendo Switch. The game completely reimagined the Zelda franchise, introducing new mechanics and a fresh story (no spoilers are in this website). However, the game also provided a perfect introduction to Zelda for people looking to get into the franchise.
